Prompt Examples

Brainstorm

  • Give me five angles for a persuasive essay on topic.
  • List pros and cons of idea for general readers.
  • Suggest three research questions about subject.

Outline

  • Draft a 5-part outline (intro, 3 body points, conclusion) about topic.
  • Turn these notes into an outline: [paste notes].

Revise

  • Simplify this paragraph without losing meaning: [paste].
  • Improve clarity and fix grammar while keeping my tone: [paste].
Nielsen (1994), WCAG 2.1 (2018), Floridi & Taddeo (2016), Raji et al. (2020)
